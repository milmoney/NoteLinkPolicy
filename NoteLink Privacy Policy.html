Privacy Policy for NoteLink Extension

This privacy policy explains how the NoteLink Chrome Extension collects, stores, and uses your data.

Information We Collect

  • Website Content: The extension allows you to store notes and links you manually input. This data is stored locally and synced to your Chrome account using Chrome's sync storage.
  • User Activity: User activity, such as actions taken within the extension (e.g., adding or deleting notes), may be stored locally for the purpose of managing your notes and links.

Information We Do Not Collect

NoteLink does not collect or store personally identifiable information, financial or payment information, authentication details, or location data.

How We Use Your Information

All information collected is used solely for the purpose of managing your notes and links within the extension. We do not share, sell, or distribute your data to third parties.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
